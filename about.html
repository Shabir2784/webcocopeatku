About CocoHarvest

Our story, our mission, and the people who make it happen.

Our Foundation

Growing Together with
Nature's Best

Founded in 2012 in Makassar, South Sulawesi, CocoHarvest Exports was born from a simple idea: transform Indonesia's abundant coconut husk waste into premium agricultural media that benefits growers worldwide.

Today we operate a 15,000 m² processing facility with the capacity to produce 5,000 metric tonnes per month, exporting to over 30 countries across Europe, North America, the Middle East, and Asia.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver consistently premium cocopeat products that empower growers worldwide to achieve better yields sustainably — while creating lasting value for our farmers, team, and the planet.

Our Vision

To be recognised as the world's most trusted Indonesian cocopeat brand — setting the benchmark for quality, sustainability, and customer service in the global growing media industry.

Our Values

  • Quality without compromise
  • Transparency in every deal
  • Sustainability at the core
  • Continuous improvement
