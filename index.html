Who We Are

Indonesia's Premier
Cocopeat Exporter

CocoHarvest Exports is a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium cocopeat products based in South Sulawesi, Indonesia. We turn coconut husks — a natural byproduct — into world-class growing media trusted by professional growers worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art processing facility is equipped with advanced washing, drying, and grading technology, ensuring every shipment meets the highest international standards for EC, pH, and moisture content.
