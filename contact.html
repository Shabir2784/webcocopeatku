Frequently Asked Questions

Our standard MOQ is 1 × 20-foot container (approx. 22–24 MT depending on product). For samples, we can send 2–5 units at courier cost. We also accommodate LCL (Less than Container Load) shipments for initial orders.

We provide: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin (COO), Phytosanitary Certificate, MSDS, and Certificate of Analysis (COA). Additional documents such as FORM E (ASEAN) or fumigation certificates are available upon request.

We accept T/T (Bank Transfer) — 30% advance, 70% before shipment. Letter of Credit (L/C) at sight is also accepted for large orders. For long-term partners, credit terms can be discussed.

Yes! We offer full OEM and private label services. You can provide your own design, or our team can help design packaging. MOQ for OEM is 1 × 20-foot container. We support custom block weights, bag sizes, and multilingual labels.
